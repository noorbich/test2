02 / MANIFESTO · READ IN 38 SEC

Strength is not a gift. It is a debt paid in sweat, in discipline, in the willingness to return to the bar when every fiber says stop. We do not sell shortcuts. We do not sell vanity. We sell the patient, brutal craft of building a body that does what you ask of it — for decades, not for summer. The coaches here have competed, certified, and failed forward. The members here have shown up at five in the morning and eleven at night. This is not a gym. This is a collective.
